--- title: "Automation steps" excerpt: "Process background
" collection: portfolio --- 1. Open fill line valve 2. Open vacuum line valve. 3. Turn on vacuum. 4. Allow 3-5 minutes to elapse to draw vacuum 5. Close vacuum line valve. 6. Open air line valve to introduce 30 psi into oil chamber. 7. Close air line valve after desired psi reached. 8. Open vacuum live valve. 9. Articulate mwd tool assembly 10. Repeat steps 6 to 9 four times. 11. Close vacuum line valve. 12. Repeat steps 6 and 7. 13. Place mwd tool assembly in final position
